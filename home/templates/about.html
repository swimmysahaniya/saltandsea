The Journey of Salt&Sea Adventures:

Where It All Began

Salt&Sea Adventures was born from the passion for the thrill of expedition and exploration ardently shared by lifelong friends and adventure enthusiasts, Sudhir,Yash and Tanvi. Founded in 2019, a simple purpose laid our company’s foundation; to offer unforgettable tour experiences that connect people with the beauty and wonder of travel. Owing to our commitment to personalised service and unique itineraries, we quickly grew from our humble beginnings of organising local yet unique escapades, into a widely trusted and recognised travel company worldwide, earning exceptional reviews from numerous satisfied adventurers. Our story is one of passion, growth, and an unwavering dedication to share the mystique joy of travel with the world and we’re excited to continue exploring new horizons with you.

What We Offer

SAIL with Salt&Sea Adventures

S - Seamless Experiences

Known for organising smooth journeys, we take care of all the details, from itinerary creation to booking, ensuring a hassle-free travel experience. Our travellers are provided with every comfort including our team of travel experts and local guides as well as meticulously tailored services like transportation, accommodations and other activities.

A - Authentic Adventures

We offer personalised experiences to our Travellers ranging from engaging with local communities and experiencing their traditions, cuisine and daily life, to exploring off- the-beaten-path destinations and hidden gems or even participating in interactive experiences such as guided hikes, city sightseeing and marine excursions.

I - Inspiring Destinations

We offer a diverse array of destinations like breathtaking coastal vistas and pristine beaches, vibrant culture of bustling cities and tranquil beauty of historic towns or thrill of untamed wilderness. What sets us apart is our personalised and sustainable travel options that fit every traveller’s wishlist while respecting and protecting the environment.

L - Lifelong Memories

We help Create lasting memories with extraordinary adventures that stay with you forever and capture special moments that make your journey truly remarkable and worth cherishing.
